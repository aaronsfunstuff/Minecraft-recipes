Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 can evaporated milk
