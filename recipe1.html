Golden Carrot Cake

Home

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup golden raisins
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the carrots, raisins, and walnuts.
  3. In another bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  4. In a third bowl, whisk together the oil and eggs.
  5. Combine all the mixtures together and pour into a greased baking pan.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let cool before serving.
